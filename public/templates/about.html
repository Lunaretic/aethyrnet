About Aethyrnet
General

    Aethyrnet is a Final Fantasy XIV: A Realm Reborn Linkshell/Free Company on the Leviathan server. Our aim is to help people clear through various content while maintining a good attitude and skill level.

LS

    Linkshell recruitment is currently open to any level 40+, and is mostly used as an LFG chat for various content. Contact Lunaretic Silva in game for an invite. There are some loose maturity restrictions, and rampant whining or complainig will likely result in you being removed from the LS.

FC

    Free Company recruitment is largely open to any level 40+ players, though we are slighly more stringent with regards to maturity. When tackling events/in-game content, FC members are peferred over LS members/others, however, we regularly fill out ranks in events with members drawn from LS or other means when lacking appropriate numbers.

Other

    Out of game communication is not required for basic particpation in either the LS or the FC, however, it is required for harder events/content in game (currently only The Binding Coil of Bahamut). While we do offer a Teamspeak server for public use by any of our members, many of our members prefer to use Skype, Steam, or other methods to communicate outside of the game, or avoid such entirely. We also have a public Free Company board on the lodestone for more long-standing communcation, as well as some communication functionality on this website.

- Lunaretic Silva
